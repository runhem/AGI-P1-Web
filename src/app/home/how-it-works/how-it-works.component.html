How to play

Blowing interface

Navigate your zeppelin by simply blowing on it. The closer you are to it, the more speed it will receive! Try to go as quickly as you can - but make sure to avoid those nasty walls!

Airdrops

When you are familiar with the navigation - look for the airdrops! The airdrops appear in the sky and contain different sizes of bounties. To achieve a highscore you have to be quick - Try to catch as many airdrops as you can before they start falling to the ground!
